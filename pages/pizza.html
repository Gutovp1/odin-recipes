Pizza Recipe (70min)

Description

Preparing a pizza from zero can seem complicated, but it is not.

Ingredients:

  • 500g Cheese
  • 500g Ham
  • 1kg Flour
  • 780g raw tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. Using a retangular dish, cover the bottom with tomato sauce, pasta, cheese and ham.
  2. Repeat three times this layer combo and finish it with sauce and oregan on top.
  3. Bake the dish in a the preheated oven.
  4. Remove the dish and wait 10min before serving.

Enjoy your meal!
